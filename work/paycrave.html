Paycrave

Mobile app allowing users to find foodcarts, order items, and pay from their phone. Designed application from sitemaps and wireframes to final prototype testing on Invision.

Find food carts nearby by default. Square pin for fixed food carts and round pin for moving food carts.

Search for food carts by category, name or location.

View in a list or on the map for a quick look into the cart.

Tapping on text gives you more information on the food cart of choice.

From the information page link to the menu to order food.
